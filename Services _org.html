IAT339 P02 WEB DESIGN

Describe

This is a website design for interior design company named: A.N.D STUDIO

Analyze

To start thinking about some essentials part of an interior design company is a crucial step. Which means we are not only simply design a website, but also design for interior design content and services. Thus, to demonstrate what kind of service that company can offer, we have to design a service list with clear hierarchy.

Evaluate

Thinking about the service list is not hard. But design a clear hierarchy for services could take time to think about it. Our group spent some time on other interior design companies' websites. We find out the solution to design a service list with a clear hierarchy. That is to state different areas with different services and then use some elements like cards to define a level of services.

IAT235 A01 INFORMATION DESIGN MOCKUP

Describe

This is a website information mock up design for City of Vancouver: Vancouver plan.

Analyze

From my design perspective, a series of data can express a lot of information from one thing. But, what does the audience sees should be visualized. By contrast, a visualized chart represents data information clearly than a single one data represented. Thus, audiences understand the information of the data as clear as the chart presented. Consequently, make data to represent information is a crucial step in the design. Also, the color of the chart can represent different information, which means using different colors to categorized different data is important in design steps.

Evaluate

I decided to use a visualized chart inside the Vancouver plan website. The reason why did I chose a webpage to deliver information, because this webpage will be inside the Vancouver plan website, and when a viewer clicks the webpage to receive information the can clearly know about the out-migration of Vancouver city, thus it's unnecessary to reach every single data website to find out data.
